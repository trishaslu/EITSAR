We are EITSAR - Empowerment through Innovative Talent Strategies and Resources.

We are an HR company that is committed to excellence. EITSAR wants to give the citizens of Baguio City fulfilling careers as well as nurturing business growth.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every individual's skills and talents are recognized and matched with the perfect opportunity. Our vision is to be the compass guiding job seekers to their dream careers and helping businesses find the right talent to achieve their goals.

What We Offer

  • Stability and Benefits : EITSAR wants to give you stability by offering you jobs that are suited to your skill set; we also want to give you benefits that ensure you are well in this ever-changing economy.
  • HR Experts : We provide insights from Baguio's very own Head of the City Human Resource Management Office, Atty. Augustin P. Laban III.
  • Strategic Talent Management : EITSAR helps businesses find the right people and talent for the job requirements that they have put down.

Our Commitment

At EITSAR, we are committed to fostering a transparent, inclusive, and equitable environment. We believe that every individual has unique strengths to offer, and we aim to bridge the gap between these talents and the organizations that need them.
