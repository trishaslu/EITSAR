F A Q S

SOME FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS TO EITSAR

FOR JOB SEEKERS

: How do I apply for available jobs?

: Contact the provided email or social media means from the employer's post.

: Can I upload my resume to EITSAR to let potential employers see it?

: We recommend that you give your resume to your potential employer only to ensure that your private and personal details are kept safe.

: Are the job listings updated?

: We ensure that the listings are constantly updated to avoid confusions or conflicts.

: What information can I add for a basic good resume?

: Here are some tips you can use for a simple but informative resume :

  • Contact Information : Include your full name, contact number, email address, and location (City and State).
  • Resume Summary or Objective : Highlight your career goals, key skills, and what you can bring to your chosen job position.
  • Work Experience/s : If applicable, list your work history in reverse chronological order, starting with your most recent job. Include your previous company's name, job title / position, dates of employment and descriptions of your responsibilities as well as possible achievements.
  • Educational Background : Include the details about your educational background. Add the name of your educational institution, degree earned, field of study and graduation dates.
  • Skills : Add a section dedicated to your relevant skills. Include your hard and soft skills: hard skills being specific technical skills that can be related to the job, soft skills are personal skills you have like communication, teamwork or problem-solving skills.
  • Achievements and Other Accomplishments : Include your awards, honors and certifications that demonstrate your skills and expertise.
  • Contact “Character” References : Add a list of people and their contacts who can attest to your character.

FOR EMPLOYERS

: I am an employer, can I seek for help from EITSAR?

: Of course! EITSAR is eager to help employers look for suitable employees by providing them with our information.

: What services can EITSAR offer for employers?

: EITSAR allows employers post their job openings and positions they have available.

: Can EITSAR do background checks?

: Unfortunately, EITSAR only helps employers post their job requirements and can not help with background checks.
